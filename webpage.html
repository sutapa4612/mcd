McDonald’s India

McDonald's is the world's leading food service retailer with more than 31,000 restaurants in 119 countries serving more than 50 million customers each day. In India, McDonald's is a joint-venture company managed by two Indians. While Amit Jatia, M.D. Hardcastle Restaurants Pvt. Ltd. owns and spearheads McDonalds in west & south India, McDonald’s restaurants in North & East India are owned and managed by Vikram Bakshi’s Connaught Plaza Restaurants Private Limited.
